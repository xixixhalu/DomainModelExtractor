The System shall be developed using ReactNative .
The System shall store persistent AccountData in a Database .
The System shall sync to a given Device 's ContactList once given Permission .
The System shall store the hashed Version of the Password in a Database .
The System shall authenticate the User using an Email and Password , allowing an Individual to sign into their Account from multiple Devices .
